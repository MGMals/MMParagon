Our Services

What We Do

Image Processing Systems

Image processing begins with the best printers, multi-functional devices, scanners, managed print services software, and display systems.

But it continues to include computer systems and storage to provide the ability to use the resulting structured and unstructured data that are the result of the front-end image processing systems.

Digital Transformation Systems

Digital transformation converts non-digital products, services, and other operations into digital systems. Paragon-One Group, LLC provides the technology to make this happen.

Once an office, corporate entity, or government agency is digitized, it can store, analyze, and manipulate the resulting data. It can also provide better remote operations for its employees, better customer service for its customers, and be more resilient to natural disasters.

Technology as a Service (TAAS)

Paragon-One Group, LLC provides TAAS to allow agencies and corporations to stretch their IT budget and improve cash flow.

Under traditional methods, for example, a company would provide all of its employees computers, printers, and other peripherals and pay for these items up front, often at the same time.

With technology as a service, the company would pay for items over a 3-year or 5-year period of time. This preserves the initial outlay of cash, provides more flexibility, and allows for upgrades as items become obsolete or become end-of-life.

Cloud Enablement

Paragon-One Group, LLC partners with Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and IBM to provide Cloud services for software and storage services. Government software deployment often requires use of FedRAMP authorized systems.

AWS, Microsoft Azure, and IBM are all FedRAMP authorized. Paragon-One is also able to enable on-site Cloud-enabled systems, hybrid Cloud-enabled systems, and off premises Cloud-enabled systems.

Cybersecurity Services

Paragon-One Group LLC teams with best-in-class cyber security software vendors to provide back-up services that protect against, ransomware attacks, penetration testing vulnerability auditing and systems auditing.
