Paragon-One Group, LLC is an Information Technology (IT) company that began business operations in 2014. We are located in the Washington D.C. metropolitan area in the town of Gaithersburg, Maryland.

We are:

  • A small business (SB)
  • A small disadvantaged business (SDB)
  • A woman owned small business (WOSB)
  • An economically disadvantaged woman owned small business (EDWOSB)

Mission

To bring the Federal Government, State and Local Governments, and Corporations into the future using the latest information technologies.

Vision

To become a world class company that delivers best-in-class solutions to its customers, and to become an ethical and honest employer that provides technology to enhance the lives of global citizens.

Paragon-One Group LLC is led by Toni K. Hines and Frederick C. Hines, two experienced experts in the IT world. Both spent many years working for companies in the Defense, Intelligence, and Telecommunications industries.


With the skills acquired working for these companies, Toni and Fred made the decision to begin reselling information technology to the U.S. Government.

Paragon-One Group’s first customer was the U.S. Defense Logistics Agency (DLA). What began as a very small one-time contract, has morphed into several multi-year contracts.
